Our Sweet Story

The passion and people behind Sweetiebell Bakery

Our Beginning

From grandmother's kitchen to your favorite bakery

Sweetiebell Bakery was founded in 2025 by Belkisa (Bells) Huseinspahic, who grew up baking alongside her mother. What started as a passion for creating delicious treats for family and friends soon blossomed into a beloved local bakery.

Bell named the bakery "Sweetiebell" as a tribute to her mothers's nickname for her. Today, we continue to honor those family recipes while adding our own creative twists to bring you the perfect blend of traditional and contemporary baked goods.

Our Values

Made with Love

Every treat that leaves our kitchen is crafted with attention to detail and a genuine love for baking. We believe you can taste the difference when food is made with passion.

Quality Ingredients

We source the finest ingredients, including locally-produced butter, farm-fresh eggs, and seasonal fruits. We never use artificial preservatives or flavor enhancers.

Community First

We're proud to be part of this community. Sweetiebell regularly donates to local charities and participates in community events to give back to those who support us.
